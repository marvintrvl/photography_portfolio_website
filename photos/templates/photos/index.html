About me

Hello,
I am Marvin, a passionate photographer based in Dresden, Germany.
Ever since I started taking pictures in 2018, I have been on an incredible journey of growth and exploration in the world of photography. It all began with my trusty phone camera, capturing moments and scenes that caught my eye. In 2019, I took a leap and invested in my first full-frame camera, which opened up a whole new world of possibilities. Since then, I have been dedicated to honing my skills and expanding my knowledge. Each year, I challenge myself to learn new techniques and push the boundaries of my creativity. I am proud to say that I am entirely self-taught, constantly seeking inspiration from fellow photographers and experimenting with different styles and genres. My goal is to capture images that evoke emotions and tell stories, whether it's the breathtaking beauty of landscapes, the raw and intimate moments of portraits, or the vibrant energy of street photography. Photography has become more than just a hobby for me; it has reached a professional level where I strive to create images that leave a lasting impact. I am always seeking new opportunities and collaborations, so if you have any questions or would like to work together, please don't hesitate to reach out to me. Thank you for joining me on this incredible journey through the lens!
